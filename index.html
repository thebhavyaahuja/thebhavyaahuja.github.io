Born and brought up in Dehradun, Bhavya has always been interested in a wide range of activities. He loves to visit new places and absorb new culture. He loves, to try new food and sleep, the latter often being a reason he misses classes (and quizzes).

He is also a first year BTech and MS by research CSD student at IIIT Hyderabad.

He is the lead guitarist of the world-famous, respected Indie rock band, called 'Loaded Dipers'. The band is really optimistic about their future, despite facing the recent rejection to perform at NIFT Hyderabad.

On the first day of his college, he fell on the basketball court and was taken to two hospitals and got four stitches over his left eyebrow. He recalls the experience as 'fun' and 'lore-building' as a form of cope to cover his poor basketball skills.

He would describe himself as an ambivert with a lot of seemingly unrealistic goals. But his current goal is to have a lot of fun in college while learning as much as possible. He also thinks writing about himself in third person is cool.
